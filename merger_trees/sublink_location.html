SubLink Trees: Location

The locations provided here are within the Odyssey computing cluster. For public data access, see the Illustris website.

The subhalo merger trees come in two varieties: dark matter and galaxies. In the first case we only track the dark matter particles, while in the latter case we consider the star particles plus the star-forming gas particles.
