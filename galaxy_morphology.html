Galaxy morphology with statmorph

Along with Jennifer Lotz and Gregory Snyder, I developed statmorph, an affiliated package of Astropy for calculating non-parametric morphological diagnostics of galaxy images, such as the Gini–M20 (Lotz et al. 2004) and concentration–asymmetry–smoothness (Conselice 2003) statistics, as well as fitting 2D Sérsic profiles. The official site can be found on ReadTheDocs.
